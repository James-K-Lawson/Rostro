Welcome to Rostro!

Tired of opening and reopening your excel roster to check when your next shift is?
Worry no more! Rostro has you covered.

Rostro is a service which easily converts your Excel roster into a .ics file. ICS is a universal calendar format used by essentially all of your home devices. It imports easily into iCalendar, Google Calendar, and Microsoft Outlook among others. Feel the ease of simply opening your phone and viewing your upcoming shifts on your phone's calendar widget!

Simply upload your roster, enter your name, and we'll process your .ics file in seconds.
