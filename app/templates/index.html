What is Rostro?

Rostro takes your Excel roster file and converts it into an .ics file.


What is an .ics file?

An .ics file is a type of calendar file used by essentially all your devices. It's used by iCalendar, Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook and many others calendar platforms.


So what's the benefit?

You can import an .ics file into your favourite calendar platform and easily be able to check your roster from all your devices whenever, and wherever you want.


How do I use Rostro?

Simply upload your roster, enter your name, and you'll receive an .ics file you can import into your favourite calendar platform in seconds. It's that easy.
